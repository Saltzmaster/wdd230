Demographics

Kenai is a city in the Kenai Peninsula Borough in the U.S. state of Alaska. As of the 2020 census, the population of Kenai was 7,748.

Events

Kenai hosts various events throughout the year, including the Kenai River Festival, the Kenai Peninsula Fair, and the Fourth of July Celebration.

Attractions

Kenai is known for its stunning natural beauty and outdoor recreational opportunities. Visitors can enjoy fishing on the Kenai River, wildlife viewing at the Kenai National Wildlife Refuge, and hiking in the nearby Chugach National Forest.
