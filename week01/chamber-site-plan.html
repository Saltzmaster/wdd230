Kenai Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

The Kenai Chamber of Commerce website aims to:

  • Facilitate business-to-business networking.
  • Provide information about local events and initiatives.

Scenarios

  1. What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?
  2. Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?
  3. What has been the population growth in the area?

Typography

Open Sans and Roboto Condensed

The website uses Open Sans for the main body text and Roboto Condensed for headings.

Color Schema

Color Schema: #115A97, #BC2A5E, #7F3B72, and white
